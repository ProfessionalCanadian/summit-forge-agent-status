# Summit Forge Labs — Brand Guide (Status Page)

## Logo
- Primary logo: oval badge, double-ring border, mountains + lightning bolt, SF monogram, "SUMMIT FORGE LABS" curved text
- Treatment: 3D embossed/forged chrome/silver metal — not flat
- Two logo files:
  - `logo-chrome-light.jpg` — bright silver chrome on carbon fiber (use on dark bg)
  - `logo-chrome-dark.jpg` — muted dark chrome on carbon fiber (more subtle)
- No transparent PNG available — use on a carbon fiber or near-black section

## Visual Identity
- **Primary background**: Carbon fiber weave texture (dark, near-black)
- **Material language**: Forged metal, embossed 3D, beveled edges, industrial depth
- **Metal finish**: Chrome/silver primary — brushed, polished, or matte depending on context
- **NOT flat**: Everything has physical depth — raised, pressed, stamped, embossed
- **Accent colors** (from wall plaque, secondary use): gold (#c9a84c), copper (#b87333)
- **Primary UI palette**: near-black (#090b10), dark chrome, silver/white text

## Tone
- Industrial precision
- High-performance, tactical
- Premium but not decorative — earned, not ornamental
- Think: machined parts, stamped steel, carbon fiber panels

## What the brand is NOT
- Not flat/minimal (no Apple-style clean white)
- Not gold-forward (gold is accent only — the main material is dark metal)
- Not corporate tech blue
- Not consumer/lifestyle
